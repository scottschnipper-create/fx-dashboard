FX Market Facts

$7.5T
Daily Trading Volume

The FX market is the largest financial market in the world with over $7.5 trillion traded daily (BIS 2022).

24/5
Trading Hours

FX markets operate 24 hours a day, 5 days a week, across major sessions: Sydney, Tokyo, London, and New York.

88%
USD Involvement

The US Dollar is on one side of approximately 88% of all FX transactions globally.

EUR/USD
Most Traded Pair

EUR/USD accounts for roughly 23% of all daily FX turnover, making it the most liquid currency pair.

180+
Currencies Worldwide

There are over 180 currencies recognized by the UN, but only about 18 are commonly traded in the FX market.

London
Largest FX Hub

London handles ~38% of all global FX trading volume, making it the world's largest foreign exchange center.
